Vrinda Store - Annual Sales Report (2022)

Key Insights:
1. Women contributed ~65% of total purchases.
2. Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh together contributed ~35% of total sales.
3. Adult age group (30-49 years) contributed ~50% of sales.
4. Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra contributed ~80% of overall sales.
5. Highest sales recorded in March.
6. Clothing category was the best-selling category.
